Biography

Hi! I’m a Ph.D. student working on deep learning, sharing my time between the Center for Data Science of ENS Paris and Facebook AI Research. Prior to that, I studied Theoretical Physics at ENS Paris, and worked with NASA on black hole mergers.

My research interests include overparametrized neural networks and their applications to computer vision and symbolic mathematics. Outside work, I love communicating about science and playing the clarinet !
