Biography

Hi! I’m a Ph.D. student working on deep learning, jointly supervised by Giulio Biroli (ENS Paris) and Levent Sagun (FAIR Paris).

My research seeks to understand the fundamentals of how neural networks learn from data. Before that, I studied Theoretical Physics and worked with NASA on black hole mergers.

I love communicating science to the general audience, and wrote a couple books for this purpose. In my spare time I also play music, feel free to check out my videos!
